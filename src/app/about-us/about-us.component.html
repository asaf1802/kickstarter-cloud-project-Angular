About Us

In a project on behalf of our studies, students of industrial engineering and management at Azrieli College, have developed a website that will serve you as a forecasting tool.
Using this tool any user who has a vision for the project will be able to enter the required project characteristics according to the template and get feedback.
The template will show him a measure of the success of the project and allow him to consider steps.
Every project matters and our goal is to create a path of self-confidence and help fulfill the vision of the initiators.

Work levels:
1. Introducing the required characteristics of the project: Main Category, Sub Category, Currency, Duration, Country and Goal
2. Waiting for feedback
3. Set out with self-belief!
